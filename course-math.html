Course Overview

Our Mathematics & Sciences program provides a comprehensive foundation in STEM subjects, preparing students for advanced studies and careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Through hands-on experiments, problem-solving activities, and real-world applications, students develop critical thinking and analytical skills essential for success in the 21st century.

What You'll Learn

Students will explore advanced mathematical concepts, scientific principles, and their practical applications. The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical understanding and practical laboratory experience, ensuring students are well-prepared for college-level coursework and future STEM careers.

Course Syllabus

Algebra & Geometry

Advanced algebraic concepts, geometric proofs, and trigonometric functions

Calculus

Limits, derivatives, integrals, and their applications in real-world problems

Biology

Cell biology, genetics, ecology, and human anatomy with lab work

Chemistry

Atomic structure, chemical reactions, stoichiometry, and organic chemistry

Physics

Mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and quantum physics

Research Methods

Scientific methodology, data analysis, and research project design
